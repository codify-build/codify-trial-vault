# Offer

- **Consulting:** $180/hr — problem-solving, diagnostics, pattern recognition

## The Value They Actually Deliver
Decision logic capture — the "if this, then that" knowledge that took 32 years to build. Not just installing systems, but encoding the reasoning that makes systems work in edge cases.

When they implement, the real deliverable isn't the automation. It's the institutional knowledge embedded in the automation — the tolerances Frank knew, the supplier quirks nobody documented, the failure modes that only experience catches.
