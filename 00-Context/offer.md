## Delivery Mechanics
- **Week 1:** I sit in every recurring meeting. I don't talk. I map the decision flow, the unspoken hierarchy, the bottlenecks. I interview each direct report privately — 30 minutes, one question: "What decision have you wanted to make but felt you couldn't?"
- **Week 2:** Decision Audit presentation. We redesign the decision rights framework together. Who owns what. What needs CEO input vs. CEO approval vs. no CEO involvement at all.
- **Week 3-4:** I attend meetings again but this time I intervene in real-time. When the CEO reaches for a decision that isn't theirs, I flag it on the spot. When a director defers unnecessarily, I redirect.
- **Month 2-3:** Spacing out to weekly check-ins. The new operating rhythm is taking hold. We track the decision distribution weekly.
