# Welcome to Codify

You're in. This vault is your business brain — everything AI needs to know about you, structured and ready to compound.

---

## What to Do Right Now

### Step 1: Enable Community Plugins
You should see a prompt asking to enable community plugins. Click **Enable** — the vault comes pre-loaded with Git sync, Templater, and Dataview.

If you missed the prompt: **Settings → Community Plugins → Turn on community plugins**

### Step 2: Explore the Sample
This vault comes with a **filled-in example** — a fictional executive coach called Marcus Webb. Open the `00-Context` folder to see what completed context files look like:

- **soul.md** — Core beliefs, origin story, contrarian positions
- **offer.md** — What you sell and how it's structured
- **audience.md** — Who you serve and what they're struggling with
- **voice.md** — How you sound, your tone, your language patterns

**These are samples.** When you run `/start`, they'll be replaced with your own business context. Take a few minutes to read through them so you know what to expect.

### Step 3: Open Claude Desktop (separate app)
Keep Obsidian open — this is where your files live. Now open **Claude Desktop** alongside it. This is a separate app where you talk to the AI.

1. Open **Claude Desktop** (download from claude.ai/download if you haven't)
2. Sign in with your **Claude Pro** account
3. Click the **"Code"** tab at the top of the window (not "Chat" or "Cowork")
4. You'll see a terminal. Type this and press Enter:
   ```
   cd path/to/codify-trial-vault
   ```
   (Replace `path/to/` with the actual folder location where you unzipped the vault)
5. Then type:
   ```
   /start
   ```
6. The AI will ask you questions about your business and fill in your vault — guided, step by step, about 30 minutes

## What Happens Next

- **/start** guides your first extraction (~30 minutes)
- The sample context files get replaced with yours (soul.md, offer.md, audience.md, voice.md)
- Every skill you run after that reads your context first
- The more you use it, the better it gets — context compounds

---

## Two Apps, One System

| App | What it does |
|-----|-------------|
| **Obsidian** | View, browse, and manage your vault files |
| **Claude Desktop** | Talk to the AI, run skills like /start, /extract, /content |

Keep both open side by side. Obsidian is your knowledge base. Claude Desktop is your AI engine.
