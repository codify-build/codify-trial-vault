## The Meta-Insight

He built a business on solving institutional knowledge loss for manufacturers — but his own 32 years of expertise is the most at-risk asset in his business. If he steps away tomorrow, his company has the same problem Frank's company had.

**He is his own best case study for why this matters. And he can't see it because he's too close.**

## The Biggest Gap

**No productised offer.** He's selling hours ($180/hr) when he should be selling the decision logic capture as a product ($25K-$50K). The implementation project is the delivery vehicle, but the actual value — institutional knowledge preserved — isn't priced, positioned, or sold as a standalone offering.

## What a Full Extraction Would Surface

A 90-minute Context Extraction interview would fill:
- Quantified proof (specific client results with dollar figures)
- Decision log (the 5-10 pivotal choices that shaped the business)
- Competitive positioning (exactly why clients choose him over the $250/hr firms)
- The full "Frank methodology" — his process for capturing institutional knowledge, documented as an IP asset
