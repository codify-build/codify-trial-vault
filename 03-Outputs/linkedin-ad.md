## Creative Direction

**Visual:** Split image — veteran employee at machine (left) / empty workstation (right). No people-heavy stock photography. Raw, industrial. The Frank story should be visible in the image without text.

**Tone notes:** See [[voice]] — no corporate speak, no "digital transformation," no "Industry 4.0." Use real language. "Frank" is a real name, not a persona label.

**Do not use:**
- "Leverage your workforce's institutional knowledge" — too consultancy
- Any mention of AI or digital twin technology in primary copy — distracts from the human knowledge angle
- Automation vendor language — we are explicitly not an automation vendor
